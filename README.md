## 📖 Description

**Melanin Beauty** is a visually captivating website dedicated to celebrating the beauty and diversity of African women. This project highlights a collection of photographs illustrating the natural splendor and unique style of African women, while honoring their rich cultures.

Through carefully curated photo galleries, **Melanin Beauty** offers a space where visitors can discover and appreciate the richness of melanin — the skin's natural pigmentation. The site captures the authenticity and uniqueness of each model, showcasing a diversity of styles, expressions, and cultural traditions.

Each image celebrates the natural beauty of African women, featuring their different skin tones, hairstyles, as well as their distinctive outfits and accessories.
